This letter has no equivalent in the English alphabet.
I will use [ kh ] to represent it.
It is somewhat harsher than the Scottish and North
German ch.
Has two pronunciations, actually both are common.
People from the plain of Nineveh use a pronunciation which is midway between h and the ordinary kh.
