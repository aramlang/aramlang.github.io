Lesson 107

Examples - 3 -


min âyda mdheeta eethâyk?
From which city are you?
 

min mdheeta dârbâ' eelu eethây
I am from the city of ârbâ' Elu
ârbâ' Elu: or Arbil as it is known in modern times.
An old city of the Assyrian Empire. It was considered the religious capital. It means 'the city of four gods'.
 
mana palâkh awukh?
What does your father (work) do for living?
 
aw eethaw mâkh-khara
My father is an engineer
 
mana 'awédh ât?
What do you do?
 
in-na eethây âsya
I am a physician
The word âsya means Physician, Doctor "title of a medical practitioner only".
 

âynaw yawma q:âdhmaya dwit:lana?
Which is the first day of the holiday?
 
âykân-na eethâykon?
How are you?  (masc. pl.)
 
âymik-ka athé ât?
Where do you come from?


ara eeth béith ârké bâq:reethakh?
Is there a library in your town?
 

ara eeth bâq:reethakh béith ârké?
Is there a library in your town?
 

én eeth bâq:reetha deel béith ârké
Yes, there is a library in my town
 

la lâyt bâq:reeth béith ârké
No, there is no library in my town
