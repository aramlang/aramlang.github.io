Pronouns

Pronoun : is a word or a single letter, sometimes a few letters, used to replace noun. It represents a person or a thing previously mentioned or known.
Sometimes, it is written as one word.

The preposition means " instead of ", or " in the place of ". Therefore, means " in stead of noun ".


Pronouns are divided into two groups:
1- Affix forms: are attached to nouns, verbs and adverbs.
2- Detached forms or Separate forms: are not attached, they stand by themselves.
