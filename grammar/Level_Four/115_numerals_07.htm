Lesson 115

Numerals

Part Seven

Distributives are expressed by repeating or doubling
the numeral.
khâdh khâdh  One each, or One at a time.
tmanya tmanya  By eights, or Every eight.
Also by means of the preposition Béith
Béith ârib'a A group of 4 together (masc.)
Béith ârbâ' A group of 4 together (fem.)
An approximate number is formed when two
numbers follow each other.
shâw'a tmanya marodhé
Seven or eight rebels
