Lesson 120

  
A dialogue

 Ashur:
breekh s:âprakh
May your morning be blessed 'Good morning'
 Sargon:
breekh wâwreekh
Blessed and blessed
 Ashur:
khâylakh alaha
May God strengthen you
 Sargon:
alaha nnât:rakh
May God protect you
 Ashur:
shlam lakh
Peace to you
 Sargon:
bshâyna w-wâshlama
'Welcome' In tranquility and in peace
 Ashur:
khdheeth sâg-ge bâkhzathakh
I rejoiced a lot by seeing you
 Ashur:
âykân-na eethâyk
How are you?
 Sargon:
tawdee lmarya, khleem na w-wâs-seem
Thank to the Lord, I am healthy and well
 Sargon:
pq:odh wâl'âs 'âm-mân
Please come and eat with us
 Ashur:
tawdee lkhub-bakh sâg-ge
Thanks a lot for your intimacy
 Ashur:
li'séth 'âdhla eéthé lsu'ranakh
I ate before coming to your visit
 Ashur:
'widh t:âybu
If you please
 Ashur:
hâwlee lkhodh
Give me only
 Ashur:
kasa dmây-ya qareeré
A cup of cold water
 Ashur: aw
Or

kasa dthirya in mâs:ya
A cup of juice if possible
 Sargon:
hân-neeäa rakhém
Enjoy it my friend
 Sargon:
imlé lakh kasa khréna (h:réna)
Should I fill another cup for you?
 Ashur:
la, kâdu lee kasa
No, one cup is enough for me
 Ashur:
tawdee, wpush bâshlama
Thank you, and remain in peace 'farewell'
 Sargon:
zil bâshlama wâq:ree shlamé lawahâyk
Go in peace and give regards 'salute'
to your parents
