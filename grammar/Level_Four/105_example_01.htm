shma drakhma deel eethaw toma
My friend's name is Thomas
 
The proper name Thomas is derived from the root verb
tïm, which means, became paired or twin.
 
eeth, There  is, There are, Has, Have
This word is indeclinable, when it stands alone is
of either gender and number.

When it is used together with the negative[ la, No], it is written as [ lâyt, There is (are) not, There is (are) no].

It takes the affixes of a plural noun and then has gender and number. Refer to lesson 96 , Group II.
