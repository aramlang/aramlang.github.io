Lesson 113

Numerals

Part Five


bnây bâytân tmané nâwshan in-nén
4 3 2 1
1   Our family
2   Eight (fem.)
3   Persons, Souls
4   Are


awa wim-ma wâthlatha âkh-khé wâthlath âkhwatha
Father and mother and three brothers and three sisters
Note: The word "Father" is pronounced "awa" and not "âw-wa"


s:ipra khdha beedha		 One sparrow in hand
(fem.)

t:awây min 'sâr		 is better than ten

s:ipreen d'âl eelana		 sparrows that are on the tree

gâwra sawa eeth wa léh		 An old man had

trén bneen khwin-nané		 two lazy sons

bsidhra deel eeth		 In my classroom there are

'isra mawtwé, khâdh kurseya		 ten seats, one chair

wâkhdha t:âwleetha		 and one table

bâr 'iwraya itheelidh
Bar Hebraeus was born

shnâth alép wmâtén w'sreen wshith
the year 1226

wâ'nâdh shnâth alép wmâtén wâthmaneen wshith
and passed away, the year 1286


Sometimes ordinal numbers are expressed by prefixing Dalath
to the cardinal numbers.


bshulam dara khmeeshaya		 By the end of the 5th century (masc.)

bshulam dara dkhâmsha		 By the end of the 5th century (masc.)

bshât-ta tleethâyta		 By the 3rd year
(fem.)

bshât-ta dâthlath		 By the 3rd year
(fem.)

pasoq:a tleethaya		 The third paragraph
(masc.)

pasoq:a dâthlatha		 The third paragraph
(masc.)

bâthlatha byârkha
On the 3rd of the month
