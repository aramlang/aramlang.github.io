Lesson 48

Rules for Aspiration

These are some of the rules about aspiration taken from Youkhanan Bar Zu'bi's grammar
(A monk who lived in the 12th century. Wrote numerous books in grammar and theology).

This lesson and the following one are merely about the rules for Q:ushaya.
Q:ushaya, as mentioned previously, is when letters retain their original sound.
Rules for Rukakha are covered in details in lessons 50-52.
