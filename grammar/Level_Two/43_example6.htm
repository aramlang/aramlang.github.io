Examples - 6 -

The teacher entered the classroom
'âl mâlpana lsidhra
  3 2 1  
1- entered: masculine, singular, 3rd person, past tense.
2- teacher: masc., singular noun.
3- to classroom: Lamâdh is a preposition = to

Aprém puts on a shirt
ârpém lawish koteena
  3 2 1  
1- Aprém: masc., proper noun.
2- put on: masc., singular, 3rd person, present tense.
3- shirt: masc., noun.

The girl combs her hair
bârtha sarq:a sâ'rah
  3 2 1  
1- girl: fem., noun.
2- comb: fem., singular, 3rd person, present tense.
3- her hair: letter Hé is a personal pronoun = her.

The man drinks water
gâwra shathé mây-ya
  3 2 1  
1- man: masc., noun.
2- drink: masc., sing. , 3rd pers. , present tense.
3- water: noun, always written in plural form.

Mariam wrote a lesson
mâryâm kithbâth drasha
  3 2 1  
1- Mariam: fem., proper noun. 
2- wrote: fem., sing. , 3rd pers. , past tense.
3- lesson: noun.

The fish swims in the river
nuna sakhé bnâhra
  3 2 1  
1- fish: masc., sing.
2- swim: masc., sing. , 3rd pers. , present tense.
3- in river: masc., Béith is a preposition = in.
