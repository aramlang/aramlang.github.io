Formation of the Plural

 Part 2

4- Adjectives (Past Participles) that start with a prefix Mim
 and ending in Yudh, form their plural by giving Pthakha to the letter that precedes Yudh.

5- Nouns starting with Rwasa and ending in Yudh form
their plural by converting Yudh into
and giving the preceding letter Sqapa.

Rwasa, as mentioned earlier, is considered as a vowel that belongs to the letter that precedes it. It should not be confused with the second letter in the following examples.
