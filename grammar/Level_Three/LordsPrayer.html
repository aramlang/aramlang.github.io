Our Father Which art in Heaven
Awoon d-Wâsh-mây-ya


Hallowed be Thy name
Nith-q:âd-dâsh Shmakh


Thy kingdom come
Téthé Mâlkuthakh


Thy will be done
Nihwé S:iwyanakh


As in heaven so on earth
Aykân-na Dwâsh-mây-ya Ap Bâr'a


Give us the bread of our need to-day
Hâw Lân Lâkhma d-Soon-q:anân Yaw-mana


And forgive us our debts
Wâsh-woq: Lân Khaw-bâyn


As we also have forgiven our debtors
Aykân-na Dap Khnân Shwâq:in L-khây-yawâyn


And lead us not into temptation
Wla Tâ'lân Lnis-youna


But deliver us from the evil
Il-la Pâs:-s:an Min Beesha


For Thine is the kingdom
Mot:ol D-dhee-lakhee Mâlkutha


And the power and the glory for ever and ever
W-khâyla w-Thish-bokhta L'alâm 'almeen


Amen
Amén
