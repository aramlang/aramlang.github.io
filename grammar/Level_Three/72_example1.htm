Lesson 72

Examples - 1 -

In the winter snow falls on the mountain.
  5 4 3 2 1  
1-in the winter: Beith is a preposition = in.
masc. sing. noun.
2- fall: verb, masc., 3rd singular, present tense.
3- snow: sing. masc. noun.
4- on: preposition.
5- mountain: sing. masc. noun.

Martha has a sweet voice
and sings a pleasant song.
  5 4 3 2 1  
  8 7 6  
1-Martha: proper fem. noun.
2- has: verb, also have.
3-her: This is the form used to express an object pronoun in close connection with verbs.
4- Voice: sing. masc. noun.
5- sweet: sing. masc. noun.
6- and sing: Wau = and,
verb fem., 3rd singular, present tense. 
7-song: sing. fem. noun
8- pleasant: sing. fem. noun.

Our garden has olives,
pears, almonds and peaches.
  4 3 2 1  
  7 6 5  
1-our garden: Nun at the end is a pronoun = our.
garden is a fem. sing. noun.
2- there is: it also means has, have, more on this later.
3- in it: Beith = in, Hé = fem. pronoun, 3rd singular.
4- olives: plur. masc. noun.
5- and pears: Wau = and, plur. masc. noun.
6- and almonds: Wau = and, plur. masc. & fem. noun.
7-and peaches: Wau = and, plur. noun.

A hunter goes hunting
and kills a deer.
  3 2 1  
  5 4  
1-hunter: sing. masc. noun.
2- go: verb masc., 3rd singular, present tense.
3- for hunting: Lamadh = for or to.
4- and kill: Wau = and - verb 3rd singular, present tense.
5- deer: sing. masc. noun.
