Examples - 3 -

The woodcutter cuts woods from the forest.
  5 4 3 2 1  
1- woodcutter: sing. masc. noun.
2- cut: verb masc., 3rd singular, present tense.
3- woods: plur. masc. noun.
4- from: preposition.
5- forest: sing. masc. noun.

The shepherd grazes the flock in the meadow,
and he has a dog,
which protects the flock from wolves.
  4 3 2 1  
  7 6 5  
  11 10 9 8  
1- shepherd: sing. masc. noun.
2- graze: verb masc., 3rd singular, present tense.
3- flock: sing. & plur. noun.
4- in meadow: Beith = in, sing. masc. noun.
5- and has: Wau = and, verb.
6- him: pronoun.
7- dog: sing. masc. noun.
8- that protect: Dalath is used to introduce a relative clause, it means = that or which.
9- flock:sing. & plur. noun.
10- from: preposition.
11- wolves: plur. masc. noun.
