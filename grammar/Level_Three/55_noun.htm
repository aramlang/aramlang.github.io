Lesson 55


Substantives

Noun: is a word that is used to name a person, place, thing, state or quality.

In this level, substantives and adjectives will be treated together since they don't differ from one another in inflexion.

The diagram shown below, illustrates the main divisions of the noun in the Aramaic language. Many examples will be provided in the next lessons to cover these main divisions thoroughly.
