Lesson 126


Reading Exercise -2-
Verses from the Holy Bible


hakhân-na kol eelana t:awa péré shâp-peeré 'awédh
So every good tree bears good fruits

eelana dén beesha péré beeshé 'awédh
But a bad tree bears bad fruits

la mishkâkh eelana t:awa péré beeshé lmi'bâdh
A good tree cannot bear bad fruits

wla eelana beesha péré t:awé lmi'bâdh
Neither can a bad tree bear good fruits

kol eelana dla 'awédh péré t:awé
Every tree that does not bear good fruits

mithpsiq: wâwnura napél
Will be cut down and cast into the fire

madhén min pérâyhon tid'un in-non
Thus by their fruits you will know them

in-nana nukhama wkhây-yé
mân dâmhâymin bee
apin nmuth nikh-khé
I am the resurrection and the life,
he who believes in me, even he die,
he shall live.

in-nana urkha wâshrara wkhây-yé
la nash athé lwath aw il-la in bee
I am the way and the truth and the life;
no man comes to my Father except by me.

kub-ba drâb min hana lâyt
dnash nâwshéh nseem khlap rakhmaw
There is no greater love than this,
that a man lay down his life for the sake of his friends
