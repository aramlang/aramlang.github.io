The Verb hwa

Verb hwa is a substantive verb "to be, to exist, to remain, to live".

When letter Hé is pronounced, it is always placed before
its predicate.
adham hwa q:rawthana
Adam became a worrier

It is also used as an auxiliary verb and in this case letter
Hé is quiescent.
q:rawthanawa adham
Adam was a worrier
