Lesson 124

The Future Tense (The Imperfect)
zâwna dâ'theedh

The different genders, persons and numbers are indicated
by prefixes together with terminations.
Singular Plural
3rd Masculine
1* nikhtow nikhtwoon
He will write They will write (masc.)
3rd Feminine
2* tikhtow nikhtwan
She will write They will write (fem.)
or, tikhtow  
2nd Masculine
2* tikhtow tikhtwoon
You will write (sing. masc.) You will write (plur. masc.)
2nd Feminine
tikhtween tikhtwan
You will write (sing. fem.) You will write (plur. fem.)
1st Common (masc. & fem.)
ikhtow 1* nikhtow
I will write (masc. fem.) We will write (masc. fem.)
1* The 1st plur. invariably coincides with the
3rd masculine singular.
2* The 3rd feminine singular invariably coincides with the
2nd masculine, singular.
Important Note
The above prefixes when added to a verbal form, take
a Zlama vowel, if the first radical of the verbal form
has no vowel.
The Four Consonants used as inflectional marks are sometimes indicated by the mnemonic wordâmnâth.


ikhtow lakh 'âl bukhrana dpilguth shât-ta
I will write to you regarding the mid-term exam.

hasha nikhtow yawnan pithghama 'âl t:ârpa
Right now, Jonah will write a verse on the paper.

'âlhadhé lawathlân âptha dnikhtow lkhon
For this, we hadn't had the opportunity to
write to you.
