Cont. Pres. = Personal Pron. + Active Participle
I am writing, I write
Continuous Past = Past of (hwa) + Active Participle
I was writing
Past Perfect = Past of (hwa) + Perfect
I had written
Frequentative Past = Past of (hwa) + Future Tense
I would write
Conditional Future Perfect = Past + Future of (hwa)
I would have written
