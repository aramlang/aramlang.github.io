Depending on your Internet connection speed, the sound may take from 10 to sometimes 20+ seconds to arrive and play.
Once the audio player comes up, the sounds can be replayed instantly, over and over with PLAY button in that window

If you are unable to hear the sound file, you need to check your browser's settings. On the Tools menu, click Internet Options, and then click the advanced tab, and look under Multimedia, make sure the option Play sounds is checked. If this didn't solve the problem, check your speakers connections or make sure your sound card is configured correctly.

If you are unable to hear the sound when using Netscape browser, again check your browser's settings. Netscape's LiveAudio plug-in is shipped with Netscape browser 3.0 and higher. It is considered the official Netscape audio player. On the Help menu, click About Plug-ins, a page will be displayed showing all the installed plug-ins. Look under LiveAudio section and see if the file with "AU" extension is Enabled.

If your settings are different from the above, and you are having no problem playing the sound files, I recommend that you do not change your browser's setting and disregard the information on this page.
