THE BOOK OF PROVERBS

Chapter 2 1-10 11-22
[ Proverbs ]


bér in tq:âb-bil mil-lây wpuq:danây tât:shé blib-bakh
My son, if you will accept my words and hide my commandments
in your heart		 1

wthis:lé idhnakh lh:ikhemtha wthâpné lib-bakh lsukala
And incline your ear to wisdom and turn your heart to understanding		 2

wthiq:ré lsukala wâlsukala treem q:alakh
And you call out for knowledge and lift up your voice to understanding		 3

wthiw'éiw âkh séma wthiws:éiw âkh seemtha
And you seek it as silver, and search for it as a treasure		 4

haydéin tistâk-kâl dikh-khelthéh dmarya wthishkâkh eedhâ'théh dalaha
Then you will understand the fear of the Lord and you will find the
knowledge of God		 5

mit:-t:ol dmaryaw yahéw h:ikhemtha wmin puméh eedhâ'tha wsukala
For it is the Lord who gives wisdom, and out of his mouth
knowledge and understanding		 6

wnat:âr sâwra lâyléin dâthrees:een wâmsây-yâ' lâyléin dâmhâlkheen
dla mum
And He stores up hope for those upright; and He helps those who walk without blemish 		7

wnat:âr urkhatha d-dheena wurkhatha dkhâsyawn nât:âr
And He guards the ways of justice, and protects the ways of the saints		 8

haydéin tistâk-kâl zâd-deeq:utha wdheena wâthrees:utha dkholhon
shweelé shâp-peeré
Then you will understand righteousness and justice and the uprightness
of all good ways		 9

kâdh tâ'-'il h:ikhemthal lib-bakh weedhâ'tha lnâwshakh twâs-sim
When you make wisdom enter into your heart and knowledge
will delight your soul 		10
