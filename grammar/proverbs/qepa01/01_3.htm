THE BOOK OF PROVERBS

Chapter 1 1-10 11-20 21-33
[ Proverbs ]


wâwrésh biryatha mâkherza wâwmâ'lané dthâr'é dkhârké mil-léh amra
She preaches in the main places; at the entrances of the
gates of cities, she says her words		 21

'dhâm-ma lim-mâth shâwré rakhmeenton shâwrutha wâm-mâyq:ané rakeenton
mâyq:anutha wsâkhlé sanéinton eedhâ'tha
How long, you simple ones will you love childishness? and the mockers
delight in their mockery, and you fools hate knowledge?		 22

in tithepnon lmâksanutha âb-bâ'lkhon rukh wâwdâ'khon mil-lâth
If you will turn to my reproof, I will pour out my spirit upon you;
and I will make known my words to you		 23

'âl dâq:réth wla hâyminton wareeméth eedh wla shmâ'ton
For I have called, and you refused; and I have stretched out my hand,
and you did not listen		 24

wshat:-ton kolhéin tare'yath wâwmâksanuth la s:wâyton
And you have despised all my counsels, and with my reproof
you had no desire		 25

ap in-na igh-h:â-kh 'âl twarkhon wikhdé ma dathé 'lâykon
dlukhyia wâwdana min shilya
I also will laugh at your calamity; and I will rejoice when a sudden
terror and destruction come upon you 		26

wâthwarkhon âkh 'âl'ala néthé wma dathé 'lâykon uls:ana w'aq:tha
And your destruction comes as a whirlwind; and when distress and
anguish come upon you 		27

haydéin niq:ronan wla i'né in-non wânq:âdmun lwath wla nishk-khunan
Then they shall call upon me, and I will not answer them, and they shall
seek me, and they shall not find me 		28

'âl dâsnaw eedhâ'tha wdhikhelthéh dmarya la gwaw
For they hated knowledge, and did not choose the worship of the Lord		 29

wla s:waw bthâre'yath wâsleew kul-la mâksanuth
They didn't desire my counsels; and they rejected all of my reproofs		 30

wikh-khâl min péré durkhathon wmin târe'yathon swâ'
And they ate from the fruits of their own ways and were filled with
their own schemes		 31

mit:-t:ol hana mâhpkhanutha dshâwré tiq:t:ol in-non wt:u'yây
dkhâs-seerây ri'yana tawbidh in-non
For the turning away of the simple shall slay them, and the error of the
weak-minded men shall destroy them		 32

wmân dshamâ'lee nishré bsâwra wnishlé min sugha dweeshatha
And he who listens to me shall live in hope, and shall refrain
from many evils		 33
