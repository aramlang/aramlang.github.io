THE BOOK OF PROVERBS

Chapter 1 1-10 11-20 21-33
[ Proverbs ]


win némrun lakh ta 'âm-mân nikhmân lâdhma wnit:sé lzâk-kaya b'i-ta
If they will say to you, come with us, let's lie in wait for someone's blood,
and let's waylay for the blameless in deceit		 11

wniwel'eew âkh shyol lkhây-yé wlâdhla mum âkh nakhtây guba
Let's swallow them, the living, as Sheol, and the blameless as
those who go down into the pit		 12

kol-léh 'uthréh weeq:aréh nishkâkh wnimlé batâyn biztha
We shall find all his wealth and precious things, and we shall fill our
houses with plunders		 13

pis:thakh arma bâynathân wkheesa khâdh nihwé lkhol-lân
Cast in your lot with us; and let us all have one purse		 14

bér la tézâl 'âmhon burkha il-la klee righlakh min shweelâyhon
My son, do not walk (go) in the way with them; but refrain your
foot from their paths		 15

mit:-t:ol drighlâyhon lweeshtaw raht:an wmistârehween lméshâdh dma
zâk-kaya
For their feet run to evil, and they are swift to shed
pure blood		 16

wâw'it-taw parseen ms:eedhatha 'âl parâkhtha
And in deceit, actually, they spread nets over a bird 		17

wkhamneen lâdhma wâmt:âsh-shéin nâwshathhon
And they lie in wait, they hide themselves to shed blood		 18

hakhân-na in-nén urkhatha dkhol d'awdeen 'awla wnasbeen nâwshatha
dmarâyhéin
Such are the ways of all who do iniquity, who take away the life
of their owners 		19

h:ekhimtha bshuq:é mishtâbkha wâwshuq:é mreema q:alah
Wisdom is glorified in the market places; and her voice is raised in
the streets 		20
