THE BOOK OF PROVERBS

Chapter 1 1-10 11-20 21-35
[ Proverbs ]


bér la niz-zâl b'âynâyk lmit:-t:âr yulpan wthârí'yath
My son, let not my commandments depart from your eyes; keep my
doctrine, and my counsels		 21

wnihwon khây-yé lnâwshakh wrukhapa ls:âwrakh
So shall they be life to your soul and grace to your neck		 22

whaydéin t-hâl-likh burkhathakh bsâwra wâwrighlakh la titq:il
Then you shall walk in your way with hope and your foot shall not stumble		 23

wthidhmâkh wla tidhkhâl wthishkâw wthiwsâm lakh shinthakh
When you sleep, you shall not be afraid; yea, you shall lie down
and your sleep shall be sweet 		24

wla tidhkhâl min dlukhya dmin shilya wmin khépa dkhât:-t:yé ma dathé
You shall not fear sudden tumult, neither the violence of the sinners, when
it comes		 25

mit:-t:ol dmarya nihwé 'âmakh wân-nât:-t:âr righlakh dla tits:eedh
For the Lord shall be with you and shall keep your foot that you may
not be caught in the snare		 26

la tithklé lmi'bâdh dshâp-peer kma dsapq:a beedhâyk lmi'bâdh
Do not withhold to do that which is good, when it is in the power of
your hand to do it		 27

wkhâdh eeth lakh la témâr lkhâwrakh dzil wtha tuw mkhar wit-til lakh
When you have something, do not say to your neighbor, Go, and come
again tomorrow, and I will give it to you 		28

la tithkhâsh-shâw beeshta 'âl khâwrakh dyathéw 'âmakh bshilya
Do not devise evil against your neighbor, seeing he dwells in peace
beside you		 29

la tdhun 'âm bârnasha b'it-ta
Do not contend with a man without a cause, if he has done you no harm 		30

wla tit:-t:ân bghâwra 'awala wla tis:bé bkholhéin urkhathéh
Do not envy a wicked man, and choose none of his ways		 31

mit:-tol dât:mâî-îu 'awala q:dham marya wshu'yathéh dmarya 'âm t:rees:é
For the wicked man is an abomination in the presence of the Lord; but the secret of the Lord is with the upright		 32

lawt:théh dmarya bwatâyhon d'awalé wdhâyírhon dzâd-deeq:é nwarikh
The curse of the Lord is in the house of the wicked; but he blesses the habitation of the righteous		 33

wniskhop lâm-mâyq:ané w'âl h:âk-keemé nrâkh-khip
Surely he mockers scorners; but he has compassion on the wise		 34

eeq:ara nértun  h:âk-keemé wsâkhlé nq:âblun s:'ra
Wise men shall inherit glory; but fools shall receive disgrace		 35
