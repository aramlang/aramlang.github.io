THE BOOK OF PROVERBS

Chapter 1 1-10 11-20 21-35
[ Proverbs ]


bér mârduthéh dmarya la tâslé wla timîân lakh bmâksanuthéh
My son, despise not the chastening of the Lord, neither be weary of
his rebuke		 11

mit:-t:ol dâlman drakhém léh marya radhé léh âkh awa dradhé lâwréh
For whom the Lord loves he disciplines, as a father disciplines his son		 12

t:uwaw lwârnasha dishkâkh h:ikhímtha wâlwâr bisra dishkâkh sukala
Blessed is the man who finds wisdom, and the son of the man who
finds understanding		 13

mit:-t:ol dt:awây téghurtah min téghurta dséma wmin dâhwa
sneena myâtran 'âl-latha
For the merchandise of it is better than the merchandise of silver,
and its gains than fine gold		 14

yâq:-q:eerai géir min képé t:awatha wmid-dim la pakhém lah
She is more valuable than precious stones; and there is nothing to be compared to her		 15

nughra dyawmatha byâm-meenah w'uthra weeq:ara bsim-malah
Length of days is in her right hand; and in her left hand riches and honor		 16

urkhathah urkhatha in-néin bâs-seematha wkholhon shweeléih shlama
Her ways are ways of pleasantness, and all her paths are peace		 17

eelanai dkhây-yé lâyléin dâmkhâmsneen bah wâyléin dâmsâk-kéin lah
t:uwâyhon
She is a tree of life to those who embrace her; and blessed are those
who wait for her		 18

marya bh:ikhímthéh sam shâthes-séih dâr'a wâthq:in shmây-ya bsukaléh
The Lord by his wisdom laid the earth's foundation; by his understanding
he set in place the heavens		 19

weedhâ'théh ithbâz-zâ' t-homé wâ'nané râs t:l-la
By his knowledge the depths are broken up and the clouds drop the dew		 20
