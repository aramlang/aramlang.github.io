THE BOOK OF PROVERBS

Chapter 3 1-10 11-20 21-35
[ Proverbs ]


bér la tit:'é namos wpuq:danây nit:-t:âr lib-bakh
My son, do not forget my law; and your heart keeps my commandments		 1

mit:-t:ol dnughra dyawmatha wâshnây-ya dkhây-yé nit-tawspan lakh
For the length of days and years of life will be prolonged for you		 2

wâshlama wt:âybutha wq:ushta la nishbq:unakh il-la q:t:or in-no
bs:awrakh wâkhthow in-no 'âl lukhé dlib-bakh
And peace, and grace, and truth will not forsake you; but bind them
about your neck; and write them upon the tablets of your heart		 3

dthishkâkh râkhmé wt:âybutha wsukala q:dham alaha wâq:dham bnâynasha
So you shall find favor, grace, and understanding in the sight of God
and men		 4

sâb-bâr bmarya min kul-léh lib-bakh w'âl h:ikhímtha dnâwshakh la titkhil
Trust in the Lord with all your heart, and rely not on your own wisdom		 5

dâ'-'ây bkholhéin urkhathakh whoo tarés: shweelâyk
In all your ways acknowledge him, and he shall direct your paths		 6

la tihwé khâk-keem b'âynây nâwshakh il-la dkhâl min marya wâst:ee
min beeshta
Be not wise in your own eyes; fear the Lord, and depart from evil		 7

dthihwé asyutha lwisrakh wdhuhana lghârmâyk
It shall be healing to your flesh and fat (nourishment) to your bones		 8

yâq:-q:âr lmarya min q:inyanakh wmin résh kolhéin 'âl-lathakh
Honor the Lord with your substance and with the first fruits of all
your crops		 9

dnithímlon aws:râyk sâw'a wmâ's:rthakh nâb'an khâmra
So shall your barns be filled with plenty, and your wine presses shall burst
out with new wine		 10
